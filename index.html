About Me

My name is Shiva Omrani. I am a computer science BS candidate at the George Washington University, and I am planning on minoring in mathematics. I work as an undergraduate researcher at the George Washington University. My current research is about election integrity, and my advising professor is Dr. Poorvi Vora. My goal for this research is to mitigate the risk associated with computerized voting system by developing government independent election audits to verify the announced election results.

During the fall 2018 semester, I will be a learning assistant for CS 2312 which is Discrete Structures II for computer science majors.
